    Video recordings¶

    Please note that the workshop will be recorded. The recording will include the following aspects of the main conferencing room:

    • Slides/Screenshares
    • Potentially videos of the speakers
    • All audio

    The recording will not include:

    • Videos of participants that are not talking
    • Any chats
    • Any conversations/videos/chats from breakout rooms.
